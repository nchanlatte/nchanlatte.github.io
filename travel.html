Top Cites Visited

Hiroshima, Japan

In 2017 I went to Japan with a group of friends. We stopped in Tokyo, Hiroshima and Kyoto, staying in cozy hostels and biking around. Every momement of the trip was breathtaking. I would return without a doubt to Japan especially Hiroshima.

Rome, Italy

During my time at Creighton I had the opportinity to travel to 5 countries with Model United Nations (by far the best club out there). My senior year we went to Rome where we had an audience with the Pope, went to dinner with Foriegn Service Embassadors for the Vatican and enjoyed amazing italian cuisine.

Nagaland, India

I spent a month in northeast India at a Jesuit school helping teach English and basic techonoligy to students. Being in Nagaland and with the Jesuits humbled me and taught me the power of knoweldge. That experience continusly reminds and inspires me to continue to give back to the community, stay educated, and never take what I have for granted.

Barcelona, Spain

I fell in love with Barcelona's food, culture and architecture. The history and design of all of Gaudi's buildings were impressive. I am still in shock at how much time it has taken to build and fund La Sagrada Familia church. Like the others on my list, this colorful and vibrant city has to say the has left an unforgettable impact on me.

Seoul, South Korea

Fast internet, spotless subways and korean bbq. Each asian culture I've visited is so unique compared to anything I've experienced. South Korea was a friendly, clean and technology advanced country. The city was surprisingly peaceful. The picture above was a train station at the DMZ that the Koreans made for when the two countries declare peace. There are no actual trains but is a symbolic place for both sides. While the South Koreans are prepared for anything, they live their day with a smile on thier face.

New York, New York

New York has been my new home since 2017! I've always loved visiting the city and was excited to make the move out here. Everyday is a new adventure with challenges and rewards. I thrive on the diversity, independance, constant change and tranquility of New York. I have yet to find a city quite like it.
